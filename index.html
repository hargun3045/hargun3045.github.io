About Me

Hi, my name is Hargun Singh Oberoi and I am a Data Science instructor.

I have a masters degree in Mathematics from BITS Pilani university.

I started my career in 2016 as a project manager at Cisco Systems, however a chance visit to a data science workshop led me to subsequently become a researcher at Harvard's StellarDNN lab.

Now, I teach introductory data science courses using python to students in India, US and China.

I'm very passionate about sports, especially football. I was the captain of our university football club and led the team in the professional league in Goa, India.

At the moment, I am founding a company to build sports-tech solutions for better training and performance for Athletes.
